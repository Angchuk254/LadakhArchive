About the Archive

Preserving Ladakh's Legacy
for Future Generations

LadakhArchive is more than a website — it's a living document that captures the essence of Ladakh. From the ancient monasteries of Alchi and Hemis to the pristine waters of Pangong Tso and Tso Moriri, from the political movements shaping its future to the educational institutions empowering its youth.

Our mission is to create the most comprehensive, accessible, and accurate resource about Ladakh — built by the community, for the world.
