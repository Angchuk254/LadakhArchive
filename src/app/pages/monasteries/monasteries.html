Lamas & Rinpoches of Ladakh

The spiritual leaders who have shaped Ladakh's Buddhist heritage across centuries.

Rinpoche ("Precious One")

An honorific for reincarnated lamas recognized as tulkus — spiritual masters reborn to continue their work of guiding sentient beings on the path of enlightenment.

Lama ("Spiritual Teacher")

A title of respect for accomplished Buddhist teachers and meditation masters who transmit teachings, perform rituals, and serve as spiritual guides for the community.

Monastic Traditions

Ladakh's monasteries belong to diverse sects — Gelug, Drukpa Kagyu, Drikung Kagyu, Sakya — each with distinct lineages of Rinpoches and spiritual practices.

Visit any monastery's detail page to discover its resident Rinpoches and Lamas — their history, teachings, and contributions to Ladakh's spiritual heritage.
