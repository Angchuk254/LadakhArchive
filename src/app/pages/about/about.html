Our Mission

Why LadakhArchive Exists

Ladakh's rich cultural tapestry — woven over millennia by Buddhist monks, Muslim scholars, Changpa nomads, Balti artisans, and countless others — is at risk of being lost in the rapid tide of modernisation and climate change.

LadakhArchive was created to build the most comprehensive, accurate, and accessible digital record of Ladakh's heritage. From ancient rock carvings at Dras to the living traditions of Changthang nomads, we document it all — so future generations can learn, connect, and be inspired.

We believe that knowledge about Ladakh should be free, community-owned, and available to everyone — whether you're a student in Leh, a researcher in London, or a traveller planning your first visit to this extraordinary land.
